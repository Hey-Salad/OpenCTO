What OpenCTO Does

A single operating layer for autonomous engineering workflows, approvals, and release safety.

Autonomous Jobs

Submit plain-language tasks. Agents plan, implement, test, and prepare PRs with checkpoints.

Human Approval Gates

Dangerous actions require explicit sign-off with traceable actor identity and evidence links.

Realtime Voice Mode

Use the audio stream view to capture requirements and generate controlled execution plans.

Built-In Compliance Signals

Policy and controls are first-class. They are evaluated before execution, not after incident.

SOC2 + DORA Controls

Checks map to control IDs and can be exported as evidence artifacts for audit packages.

Risk Warnings

Entitlement and policy warnings appear inline before unsafe actions are attempted.

Secret + Policy Blocks

Security findings and policy violations block deployment paths until resolved.

Cloud-Native Control Plane

OpenCTO integrates a Cloudflare worker backend, typed frontend contracts, and agent execution with hardened approval pathways.
